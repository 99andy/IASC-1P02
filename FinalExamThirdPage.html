Frequently Asked Questions

Is Loco Coffee safe to drink if I am lactose intolerant or have a gluten allergy?

-Loco Coffee is 100% dairy free and naturally gluten free.

Do I have to keep Loco in the fridge?

-Yes. Because the coconut water makes it a juice product, it needs to remain refrigerated. High
pressure processing allows us to extend the shelf life by preventing the growth of bacteria, but
coconut water can spoil like other fresh squeezed juices if not kept refrigerated.

Is it bad to see separation in the Latte?

-No. Loco Latte uses organic coconut milk, which will naturally separate when left still for a
period of time. The natural fats from the coconut milk harden when they are cold and still, but
they will mix right back in when given a strong shake. (Try shaking, then take a sip and shake
again when there is more room for the liquid to move in the bottle)

How much caffeine is in each bottle?

-Each bottle of Loco contains about 120mg of caffeine, which is more than a cup of coffee.
